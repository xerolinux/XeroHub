## The Ask

If you review Linux distros, I will send you a free ISO. **XeroLinux** is rare in review queues, largely because the ISO sits behind a contribution wall. This post is the workaround.

The T.U.I. installer remains free for everyone. The ISO is the polished path, normally a one-time contribution. For reviewers, both are on the house.

## How to Reach Out

Pick whichever works for you:

- [**Fosstodon**](https://fosstodon.org/@XeroLinux) (DM)
- [**Discord**](https://discord.xerolinux.xyz)
- [**Email**](mailto:steve@techxero.com) (`steve@techxero.com`)

Send the link to your channel or platform. You get a private download link for one or both ISOs.

## What I Want from You

Honest coverage. Not a promo, not a hype reel. Praise where it lands, criticism where it earns it. If you want a walkthrough of the toolkit, Kernel Manager, or any of the in-house tooling before you record, I will set aside time to walk you through it.

## Why It Matters

Visibility drives contributions. Contributions cover the mirrors, the build pipeline, and the cost of keeping a rolling Arch derivative shipping. Reviews from outside the existing community are the most efficient way to reach new users who would never otherwise hear about the project.

A free ISO in exchange for an honest review is a fair trade for both sides.
